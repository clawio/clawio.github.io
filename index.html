“We don't believe in companies, we believe in protocols. Companies appear and disappear but protocols always remain”

Based on CERNBox

CERNBox project at CERN settled the design bases for scalable sync and share servers. ClawIO has been built from scratch based on its solid principles.

Trust in open protocols

ClawIO is not just a set of specifications. It will be reference implementation of open standards like OpenCloudMesh and a future Open Sync Protocol.

Implemented in Go!

PHP does not offer advanced features related to concurrency and paralelism. ClawIO is being developed with Go to achieve incredible performance.
