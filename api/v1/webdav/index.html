WebDAV

ClawIO offers WebDAV support.

Access to the core APIs using WebDAV is possible due to new WebDAV APIs:

  • StorageDAV API: access to resources from the Storage API.
  • LinkDAV API: access to public links from the Link API.
  • ShareDAV API: access to internal shares from the Share API.
  • FedShareDAV API: access to federated shares from the FedShare API.

ClawIO offers support not just for accessing your resources in your home directory, but also all your links and all the shares. This fact makes ClawIO unique.
